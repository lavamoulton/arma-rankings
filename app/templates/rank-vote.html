How it works

In an attempt to have quicker and more balanced #pickup fort6 matches, we are creating a ranking system based on community input. The voting process generates a matchup between two random players in our active user base for you to chose from based on your perception of skill. This data will help us build the a fair match-matching algorithm, so we ask you to please be fair and responsible while voting! All data is anonymous. Cast as many votes as you'd like.
