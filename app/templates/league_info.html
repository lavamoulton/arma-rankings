How to join

If you would like to play in rated matches and see your ranking here, please join us on the armagetron discord, in the #competitions channel. Simply show up in that channel an hour to five minutes before a scheduled match (see schedule below) and post your tron name followed by login, for example 'player1 player1@forums' and you're signed up! More details can be found in this post on the armagetron forums.

League rules

The goal of having leagues and rankings is for it to be fair and fun for everyone. As such, there need to be some rules in place.

  • If you leave at any point and don't show up again, you will have a score of 0 for that match which counts towards your ranking.
  • If you do not show up on time, you won't be allowed to play.

Questions, comments or feedback?

If you have any questions, comments or feedback, we would be happy to hear from you. Please reach out by posting in the #competitions discord channel, responding to the forums post linked above, or reaching out directly to Titanoboa or raph. Thanks!
